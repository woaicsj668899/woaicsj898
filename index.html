Our Environmental Actions

Waste Classification

Promote waste sorting concepts, guide residents to correctly classify household waste, reduce environmental pollution, and improve resource recycling rates.

Tree Planting & Afforestation

Organize regular tree planting activities to restore ecological vegetation, improve air quality, and create a better living environment for wildlife.

Water Resource Protection

Carry out water source protection initiatives, promote water conservation knowledge, supervise water pollution behaviors, and protect precious water resources.

Plastic Reduction Campaign

Advocate the use of biodegradable environmentally friendly materials, reduce the use of disposable plastic products, and organize plastic waste cleanup public welfare activities.

Clean Energy

Promote the use of clean energy such as solar and wind power, advocate low-carbon lifestyles, and reduce carbon emissions.

Environmental Education

Conduct environmental protection lectures in schools and communities, raise public environmental awareness, and cultivate green living habits.

Green Living Initiative

Start with Small Daily Actions, Practice Green Living

  • Save Water: Turn off faucets immediately after use, reuse water, and use water-saving appliances
  • Save Electricity: Turn off lights when leaving rooms, use energy-efficient appliances, reduce standby power consumption
  • Green Travel: Prioritize walking, cycling or public transportation, reduce private car usage
  • Save Food: Order appropriately, eliminate waste, and practice the "Clean Plate Campaign"
  • Reuse: Bring your own shopping bags and water cups, reduce use of disposable products
  • Rational Consumption: Buy eco-friendly products, reject excessive packaging, support second-hand transactions
